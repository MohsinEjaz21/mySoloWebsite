About Us

PakChat is started as an alternative to WhatsApp for Community.Our product now supports sending and receiving a variety of media: text, photos, videos, documents, and location, as well as voice calls. Our messages and calls are secured with end-to-end encryption.

A free social networking site for Chatting, audio and vedio calling , It provides group online chat. It gives Admin authority in group communication, hence conformance to standards in buisness. Also It has location wise preference to chat as well which provides extra Security
